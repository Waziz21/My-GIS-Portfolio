    My name is Waddan Aziz.

    I'm a Geodesy Engineering graduate from Universitas Lampung with hands-on experience in surveying, mapping, and spatial data analysis. My main focus areas are GIS, remote sensing, aerial mapping, and cadastral surveys. During my internship at the National Land Agency (BPN) in Lampung Timur, I worked on a range of cadastral and thematic mapping projects.

    I’ve also been involved in fieldwork and mapping efforts across several regions in Lampung — including a road data survey in Lampung Utara, and aerial mapping for the Kunjir Beach tourism zone and geothermal areas in South Lampung. On the research side, I conducted a land cover change prediction study in SWP Cigombong, Bogor Regency — combining spatial modeling and remote sensing techniques. I’m especially skilled in GIS data analysis, photogrammetry processing, and field-to-desk mapping workflows. I enjoy working on projects that connect data with real-world spatial planning and land management challenges.
